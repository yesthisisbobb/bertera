About Us

PT BERTERA NIAGA GLOBAL

Established in 2025, PT Bertera Niaga Global is an Indonesian company specializing in the export of premium coffee commodities. As both producer and exporter, we are committed to delivering high-quality green beans and roasted coffee to the global market.

Our product range includes Arabica and Robusta varieties, with a focus on specialty beans from Mount Arjuno in East Java and the Gayo Highlands in Aceh. Our Arjuno coffee, a best-seller, undergoes meticulous processing to ensure a distinctive flavor profile that reflects the rich volcanic soil and unique microclimate of the region.

At PT Bertera Niaga Global, we embrace sustainable agroforestry practices, working closely with local farmers to preserve the environment and promote biodiversity. Our dedication to quality and sustainability ensures that every cup of our coffee offers an inspiring experience.

Driven by a passion for coffee as a catalyst for creativity, our team is inspired daily by the farmers and communities we collaborate with. We are committed to sharing the unique flavors of Indonesian coffee with the world.
